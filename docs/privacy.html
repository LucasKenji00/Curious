Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Curious ("the app", "we") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Information We Collect

Curious is designed to work entirely on your device. The app stores the following information locally on your device only, using standard mobile storage (AsyncStorage):

  • Your name (optional, provided during onboarding, used only to personalize greetings within the app)
  • Your preferences (selected topics/categories, notification frequency, and preferred notification times)
  • Curiosities you save (bookmarked content)
  • Your streak data (which days you opened the app, used to show your daily streak)

None of this information is transmitted to us, to any server, or to any third party. We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels of any kind.

Notifications

If you grant permission, Curious schedules local daily notifications directly on your device using your device's operating system. No notification content or schedule is sent to or generated on a remote server.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties, because we do not collect or have access to it in the first place. If you use the in-app "Share" feature, the content you choose to share is handled by the third-party app you select (WhatsApp, X, Instagram, or your device's native share sheet), subject to that app's own privacy policy.

Your Control Over Your Data

  • You can edit or reset your preferences at any time within the app.
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app.
  • You can revoke notification permissions at any time via your device's system settings.

Children's Privacy

Curious does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require account creation, email, or any personally identifiable information beyond an optional first name stored locally on-device.

Changes to This Policy

If Curious introduces features that involve a server or account system in the future (for example, syncing data across devices), this policy will be updated accordingly before that feature becomes available, and the date above will reflect the change.
