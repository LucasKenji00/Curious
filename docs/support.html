Need help with Curious? Start with the FAQ below — or just email us.

How do I change my notification frequency or topics?

Open the Profile tab inside the app and tap on "Categories" or "Frequency" to update your preferences at any time.

Notifications aren't showing up

Make sure Curious has notification permissions enabled in your device's system Settings. On iOS: Settings → Notifications → Curious. On Android: Settings → Apps → Curious → Notifications.

How do I delete my data?

Curious doesn't have an account system — everything lives only on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all your data, since nothing is stored on a server.
